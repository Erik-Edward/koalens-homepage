Request for Account and Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your KoaLens account and all associated data we have stored, please follow the instructions below.

Please send an email to:
koalens.app@gmail.com

Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

To help us identify your account, please include the email address associated with your KoaLens account in your message. If you are sending from a different address, make sure to clearly state the email address your account is registered with.

We will process your request as soon as possible and send a confirmation once the deletion is complete. The process may take up to a few business days.
