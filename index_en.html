Get to know KoaLens

What is KoaLens?

KoaLens uses AI and video to instantly analyze ingredient lists directly from the packaging – completely without barcodes. Get immediate answers on whether a product is vegan and avoid uncertainty.

For whom and where?

Perfect for vegans, those curious about plant-based options, or anyone avoiding specific animal ingredients. Use KoaLens in the grocery store, at home in the pantry, or whenever you need a quick check on the go.

Language is no barrier

Traveling abroad? Found an exciting imported item? KoaLens reads ingredient lists in any language and gives you the analysis directly in English*. Bring peace of mind wherever you are.
